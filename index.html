How to Create a Google Form

Step-by-step instructions for teachers

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Go to Google Forms: Visit Google Forms.
  2. Sign in: If prompted, sign in with your Google account.
  3. Create a New Form: Click on the "+" icon to create a new form.
  4. Title Your Form: Enter a title and description for your form.
  5. Add Questions: Use the "+" button to add questions. You can choose from various types like multiple choice, checkboxes, etc.
  6. Customize Your Form: Change themes and colors using the palette icon.
  7. Preview Your Form: Click the eye icon to see how your form will look to respondents.
  8. Send Your Form: Use the "Send" button to share your form via email, link, or social media.
